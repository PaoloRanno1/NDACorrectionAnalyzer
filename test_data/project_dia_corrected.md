																												§ 1 Confidential Information 

																												

Confidential information within the meaning of this agreement includes all mutually disclosed information about the investment process, the companies involved, their products and processes as well as all documents and materials provided directly or indirectly as part of the order/project processing. Furthermore, the non-disclosure agreement applies to all developments, presentations, studies, findings and results. 



The only exceptions to the agreement are information and findings that (a) are already public knowledge and therefore no longer subject to protection ++ or becomes generally available to the public through no breach by Information Recipient of the confidentiality undertakings hereunder; (b) was in Information Recipient’s lawful possession before receipt from Information Discloser, without any obligation of confidentiality owed to the Information Discloser; (c) was received in good faith by Information Recipient from a third party that is not subject to an obligation of confidentiality owed to Information Discloser; or (d) was disclosed by Information Recipient pursuant to the written permission of a duly authorized representative of the Information Discloser .++



++The Information Recipient may make disclosures required by court order or other legal obligation, provided the Information Recipient gives the Information Discloser prior written notice, uses reasonable efforts to limit disclosure and to obtain confidential treatment or a protective order, and has allowed the Information Discloser to participate in the proceedings.++ 

																												§ 2 Declaration of commitment

																												

The Information Recipient undertakes to keep all confidential information strictly secret and not to use or exploit it or pass it on to third parties without express written consent. However, the Information Recipient is free to share the confidential information with their consultants , ++ directors, shareholders, potential syndicate members, professional advisors (“Other Recipients”) ++ as long as they also commit themselves to the confidentiality of the data. 



In order to ensure confidentiality, the Information Recipient further agrees to provide their --employees and staff -- ++ Other Recipients ++  with information only to the extent necessary for the performance of the activities and to ensure that these persons also sign a corresponding confidentiality agreement, unless they are obliged to maintain business confidentiality.



++ This non-disclosure agreement does not impose an obligation on the Parties to proceed with any collaboration ++ . 

																												

																												§ 3 Duration

																												

																												The non-disclosure agreement shall apply for the duration of the collaboration and ++ , in the event of an earlier termination of the collaboration, ++ for a period of two years ++ after signature of this non-disclosure agreement ++ -- thereafter. The non-disclosure agreement also does not expire if the collaboration does not happen or has to be terminated prematurely.--

																												

																												§ 4 Liability 

																												

The Information Recipient shall be fully liable for all damages resulting from a breach of contractual obligations.
